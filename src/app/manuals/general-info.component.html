Digital Cargo Securing Manual is a browser-based portal system, where you can view your vessel's class approved Cargo Securing Manuals and other related documents.

The portal will utilize browser cache for offline capabilities, so please keep cache uncleaned.

Few notes below:

  • When the portal is connected to the web, latest documents will be uploaded automatically
  • The portal works offline; all files are accessible during long sea voyages.
  • Additionally, all documents are available for download; please store important files to a separate computer.
